About me

Hello, my name is Matilde Coelho and I am a portuguese illustrator and artist. I'm currently working as a full time illustrator since mid 2020 and my goal is to expand my portfolio and eventually open comissions to public.

My school education as an artist began in high school where I concluded my specialized Visual Arts course and enrolled in Lisbon School of Education college, at Polytechnic Institute of Lisbon, where I graduated in Visual Arts and Technology.

I have skills in many different artistic fields such as Painting, Sculpture, Drawing, Photography, Graphic Design, Product Design, Interactive Design, among others, however, I intend to focus in Drawing.

I draw in both traditional and digital media, although I prefer digital drawing. I create characters and creatures and seek to perfect my style and techniques to work in areas such as Character Design and Concept Art.
