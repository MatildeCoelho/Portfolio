Drawings

Since very young, I had an interest in drawing, however, I only began to explore that area deeper around when I was 16 years old when I decided to specialize in Arts. Although, it wasn't until I was 20 years old that I started to think about my future in this field and I found my passion for digital drawing.

The freedom to choose between countless brushes, pencils, colours, textures, among others, to draw and erase as much as I wanted and not worrying about material costs were some of the reasons that made me deepen my affection for digital drawing.

Even though I prefer digital media, I feel more at ease sketching on paper with a mechanical pencil, since my drawing tablet is a small Wacom Intuos Pen & Touch and I can only see what I'm drawing on my computer's monitor, so I never really leave traditional drawing behind.

On this page you can find some of the drawings I chose to highlight.
