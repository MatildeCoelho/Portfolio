3D Works

On my second year of university I learned how to work with certain software such as Autodesk AutoCAD, Blender 3D and Autodesk Fusion 360 and it was from my Digital Representation course that I awakened my interest and taste for creating characters and objects in 3 dimensions, specifically on Blender 3D.

The fact that you can design these models and use them in a space and environment, in animation or even bring them to life through 3D printing was also something that fascinated me quite a lot and encouraged me to create.

On this page you can find some of those projects, which I believe are the ones that represent better my skills at 3D modelling.
