Photography

Photography was always something present on my everyday life, sometimes capturing even the simplest things like a sunrise or a sunset from my window, a light reflection or a landscape were always images that I liked to capture and save, however, it wasn't until my first year of university when I truly learned how to work with a DSLR camera.

From the aperture to the photometer, from ISO to white balance, all those components that should be taken into consideration on the moment the shutter is closed and even after, in editing, were contents that I retained and nowadays keep in mind when I photograph with a camera. I always prefered to photograph places or things, however, I began to develop a liking for portraits and the human figure when I realized all the characteristics behind a photography.

Even when I don't carry my camera with me, I try to register moments in the best possible way. On this page it is possible to find some of those photos that I took, both professionally and personally.
